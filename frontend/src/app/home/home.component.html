What is BitFlow?

BitFlow Finance is a stable swap protocol designed to reward Bitcoin holders

BitFlow enables traders to swap between price-similar assets on the Stacks blockchain (such as xUSD/USDA or different versions of wrapped Bitcoin). These traders pay fees to the protocol, which are then proportionally claimed by liquidity providers. Unlike centralized services, BitFlow never takes custody of your hard-earned Bitcoin.
